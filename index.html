Our History

Founded in 1916, Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, commonly referred to as BMW, has a rich and storied history. Our journey began with the production of aircraft engines during World War I. After the war, we shifted our focus to motorcycles and eventually to automobiles.

Throughout the decades, BMW has become synonymous with luxury, innovation, and performance. We have consistently pushed the boundaries of engineering and design, leading to iconic models like the BMW 3 Series, 5 Series, and the M Series.

Today, BMW is a global leader in the automotive industry, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the sheer joy of driving. We invite you to explore our history and join us in shaping the future of mobility.
